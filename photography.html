Ramblings about my gear

How two manual focus prime lenses made me sell my kit lens

f/2.8 Zoom lenses and the only thing I don't like them for

  • Great for landscapes, portraits, and general "Photowalking"
  • A costly investment
  • Street phtography with autofocus in crowds is a pain
  • A manual prime lets me "Set and Forget" Aperture and focus
  • I still don't have a good telephoto zoom
