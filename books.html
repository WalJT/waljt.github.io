Book Recommendations

These are some books I've read that really stuck in my mind for one reason or another.

The Hussite Trilogy by Andrzej Sapkowski

These three books, namely "The Tower of Fools", "Warriors of God", and "Light Perpetual" have some of the best character development throughout the series that I have ever come across.

What If? by Randall Monroe

The author is a cartoonist who compiled answers to questions his fans submitted. Good amounts of humour and science, and planted firmly in the good clean fun category.

And All the Kings Men by Gordon Stevens

A look at what could have happened if Germany successfully invaded Britain during the second world war. A somewhat dark speculative fiction that makes a great spy thriller.
